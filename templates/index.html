This blog provides a detailed account of the design and development process of the "Sydney Life Aid" - a task-oriented community network application. This application aims to assist Chinese international students in Sydney in accessing medical assistance, locating suitable places, and learning from others' experiences. This blog elaborates on how the project evolved from its initial concept to a testable prototype.
